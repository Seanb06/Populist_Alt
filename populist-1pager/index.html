About Us

Sean Bumgarner

Co-Founder 

Sean is a New York City based product developer who has worked with major media brands creating solutions for content in the digital space for the last decade. As Chief Digital Officer at Galvanized he lead product development and monetization for multiple websites and apps. As well, he consulted with brands like Polo Ralph Lauren and Degree on mobile app development. Previously as Design Director, Platforms and Devices at Condé Nast's Editorial Development Group, Sean worked with brands like GQ, Vogue, Vanity Fair, Glamour and The New Yorker refining approach and anticipating industry developments. As the Interactive Design Director at Rodale Inc. he received several awards for his work on the Men’s Health and Best Life iPhone editions.

Carrie Kengle

Co-Founder

Carrie Kengle is a self-taught software and hardware engineer. After receiving her degree in Political Science, she began teaching herself how to program at a pioneering handheld computer software distributor. Having found a passion for working with machines, she continued as a lead developer at advertising agencies including Sapient, JWT, and R/GA in New York, London and Tokyo. In 2008, she began to explore interacting with machines beyond web and mobile. These explorations led to incorporating gesture recognition and other sensor-based interactions in installations for globally recognized clients. Inspired by the possibilities in human-computer interaction, she continues to teach herself and others about connected devices, computer vision, and robotics, and is currently an entrepreneur in residence at 10xBETA, part of New Lab. Her work has won awards at Cannes, One Show Interactive, and the London International Awards.

Populist

Populist is a user generated list creation web app that encourages users to create, save and share lists of their favorites. Lists could be places, products, restaurants or even ideas. These lists can then be liked or saved by other logged-in users.

We offer a space to create and share favorite content. Offering indexed and savable lists rated by popularity we are able to add relevancy to searches for the best products and experiences. Lists are the way we group and organize our thoughts and Populist is a solution for anyone who has an opinion on the best of anything or wants to find the best of anything.
